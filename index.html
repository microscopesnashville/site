ABOUT US

Microscopes Nashville is the premier microscope and ophthalmic equipment service and repair company. We provide the absolute finest care and maintenance of your equipment, using our expertise to keep your equipment in pristine working order for your clinical or research purposes. Microscopes Nashville services medical, clinical and university labs in the Southeast and we are committed to and grateful for our long list of satisfied clients. Microscopes Nashville CEO, Jackie Simons is a 20 year Nashville resident who is not only a brilliant microscope and ophthalmic technician but is also a music theory teacher in Nashville. He has an electronics background and was trained as a machinist. His father was a jet mechanic exposing Jackie to all things fast and precise – circuit boards and gear ratios. As far as music goes, Jackie says “it’s all just math, everything is math – lenses, light, sound and electricity.” From having his car worked on to having a refrigerator or appliance repaired, Jackie saw that there was a void of service in general. This was and is the catalyst and business model for Microscopes Nashville. A dedication to excellence and providing conscientious professional service to all our clients is who we are.

Who We Are

Microscopes Nashville services all brands and types of clinical and research microscopes and ophthalmic equipment. Restoration, calibration, cleaning and yearly preventative maintenance are our expertise. We calibrate every microscope to OEM standards and return microscopes to their best possible working order.
Brands include:
Leica, Zeiss, Olympus, Nikon, AO, Fisher, Reichert, Accuscope, Unitron
Types of Equipment Include:
Refractors, Phoropters, Slit-Lamps, Indirects, Visual Field, Bright Field, Inverted, Fluorescent, Surgical, Stereo Dissecting, Phase Contrast, DIC, Motorized, Manual, Software driver application.

What We Do

Microscopes Nashville provides immediate response, service and repair to our clients. We service and repair all brands and types of microscopes and ophthalmic equipment on-site, coordinating with lab managers and exam room schedules to provide uninterrupted patient or research flow. Equipment is professionally cleaned and adjusted to OEM standards. We also calibrate lab equipment for unique or special applications.
